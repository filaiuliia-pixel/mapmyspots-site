MapMySpots Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you use @mapmyspots_bot on Telegram, we store your Telegram User ID and the places you save through the bot. This is necessary for the service to work — specifically for the /myplaces feature.

When you visit mapmyspots.app, we use Google Analytics (GA4) and Microsoft Clarity to understand how people use the site. These tools collect anonymised usage data and may set cookies.

Payments are processed by Gumroad. We do not store your payment details.

What we don't collect

We do not collect your name, phone number, email or location unless you provide it voluntarily.

How we use your data

Solely to provide the MapMySpots service. We do not sell, share or transfer your data to third parties.

Data retention

Your saved places are stored until you delete them or request account deletion.

Your rights

You can request deletion of all your data by messaging @mapmyspots_bot with /deletedata or emailing us.
