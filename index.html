FAQ

What is MapMySpots?

A Telegram bot that turns travel recommendations into clickable Google Maps links.

What can I send to the bot?

Any screenshot or text that contains place names. Travel guides, TikTok lists, Instagram recommendations, notes, copied lists or forwarded messages. Just send a screenshot or paste the text.

Do I need to organize the places first?

No. No formatting needed. No need to extract places manually. MapMySpots finds and maps them for you.

What languages does it support?

Any language — Russian, English, Japanese, Chinese and more. Tested and working.

Can it read TikTok and Instagram?

Yes. Screenshot any travel content and send it to the bot.

Can I export places to Google My Maps?

Yes. Use /csv to export your places for Google My Maps.

Can I find places later by city?

Yes. Use /myplaces to browse saved places by city anytime.

How much does it cost?

3 free uses. Then $19 lifetime access. No subscription.
